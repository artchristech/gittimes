The CLI remains central: kortix init scaffolds a project; kortix ship deploys it; kortix sessions new launches agent workflows. Unlike chat-based AI tools, Suna produces tangible artifacts—deployed work, PRs, documentation—because the company is the repo. Ownership is total: no vendor lock-in, no black boxes, just TypeScript-defined agents and a Git history that doubles as an audit trail.

For builders, this means AI that doesn’t just converse but executes—within guardrails they define, in environments they control, with every action traceable to a commit.
